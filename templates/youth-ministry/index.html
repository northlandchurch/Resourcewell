Our hope is to help students develop a stronger relationship with Christ as they make their faith their own. These series have been created to provide a clear, easy outline for group meetings. Each lesson, complete with a leader's guide, includes a list of materials needed, opening activities, Bible engagement suggestions and interactive learning exercises. Adapt these materials in any way that is helpful for your family or ministry. It is our prayer that these studies will facilitate the development of healthy spiritual habits, as students read the Bible and see that it is applicable to their personal lives.
